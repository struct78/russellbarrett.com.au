URBAN - CONTEMPORARY, CLASSIC, LIVEABLE

With a diverse portfolio of urban residences, Russell Barrett Architects has won a reputation for creating individual houses to suit all budgets.

Specialising in sensitive and innovative renovation to houses from the Victorian era, the 1920s through to the 1950s, many with established garden to be married with new design.

New builds include family residences, townhouses, multi-housing, pool houses, pavilions, and the more playful follies and cubby houses, even garden sheds.
